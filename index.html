DOCUMENTATION ABOUT US DISCORD GITHUB

RTByte

RTByte is a fully modular multipurpose Discord bot built on the incredible Klasa framework for Discord.js. It brings a ton of features to help you run and manage your server, and is fully integrated into Discord's audit log system, allowing you to see exactly when and why the bot does something.

RTByte is a rewrite and repurposing of ChopBot -- a Discord bot written for the Rooster Teeth Family of community Discord servers.

Our goal

We aim to provide a reliable, trustworthy Discord bot with a good set of moderation tools for your use.

Should you choose to use RTByte, you'll be welcomed by our intuitive and easy to use server dashboard, that not only let's you configure every part of what the bot should do on your server, but also provides you and your team with useful server information, statistics and more.
