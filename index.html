RTByte

RTByte is a fully modular multipurpose Discord bot built on the incredible Klasa framework for Discord.js. It brings a ton of features to help you run and manage your server, and is fully integrated into Discord's audit log system, allowing you to see exactly when and why the bot does something.

RTByte is a rewrite and repurposing of ChopBot -- a Discord bot written for the Rooster Teeth Family of community Discord servers.

Our goal

We aim to provide a reliable, trustworthy Discord bot with a good set of moderation tools for your use.

Should you choose to use RTByte, you'll be welcomed by our intuitive and easy to use server dashboard, that not only let's you configure every part of what the bot should do on your server, but also provides you and your team with useful server information, statistics and more.

Features

  • Easy setup

    Fully automated setup. All channels and necessary roles are created and configured, including channel permissions.

  • Extensive logging

    Fully configurable logs for your server. Choose what you want included in your server log, and where you want the logs posted.

  • Server moderation

    Efficient moderation actions that properly log reasons to your server's audit logs (if applicable). All moderation commands obey the role hierarchy, so moderators can't act on other moderators or admins and the like.

  • Word blacklisting

    Server-configurable word blacklists that can optionally scan usernames and nicknames, issue automated warnings as well as delete messages or change nicknames.

  • Joinable roles

    Allow users to join specified roles on your server. Command can list available roles and how many people have that role.

  • Fun commands

    Various fun commands such as 8ball, dice, coinflip, etc.
